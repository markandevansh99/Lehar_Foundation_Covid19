PROMOTING VENTURES OF THE HEROES

Acid-attack survivors brew success stories in many fields professionally. The spotlight is on the Powerful Men and Women who, despite the humiliation and attacks, are shining through with their grit and determination to move forward in life.



* SHEROES HANGOUT :-


A socio-entrepreneurial project established is a collaborative effort between survivors and Delhi-based NGO, Chaanv Foundation who chose to name it 'Sheroes' as a tribute to the courage of survivors.

Check out their website



When the Sheroes Hangout cafe was first started in Agra, it opened with brisk business and supportive crowd, and its popularity now has led to its inaugural in many other cities of the country. Let us now explore them a little bit.



1. Sheroes Cafe, Agra:-




The first ever branch of SHEROES HANGOUT. The cafe, sits in the shadow of the Taj Mahal and attracts a steady stream of foreign tourists. Also, survivors creations will be displayed at a boutique inside the cafe. The two-floor cafe, which will rehabilitate five survivors, will also house a reading section containing books and journals on women empowerment.

Address :- Behind Go Stops Hostel, Taj Nagari Phase 1, R.K. Puram Phase-2, Tajganj, Agra, Uttar Pradesh 282001

DO CHECK THIS OUT NEXT TIME YOU VISIT AGRA!



2. Sheroes Cafe, Lucknow:-




The Cafe, run entirely by acid attack survivors provides a variety of scrumptious food like fast food, continental food, and beverages. People love the hospitality at Sheroes. Ajay Kumar Patel, who founded the Red Brigade, stated that that the sole aim of starting the cafe is to enable acid attackers in owning and running the cafe, where the chefs, waiters, and the billing personnel will be acid attack survivors.

Address :- Opposite Dr. B R Ambedkar Park, Vipin Khand, Gomti Nagar, Lucknow, Uttar Pradesh 226010

DO CHECK THIS OUT NEXT TIME YOU VISIT LUCKNOW!



3. The Orange Cafe, Varanasi:-




The Orange cafe is the third such restaurant that has opened in Varanasi, after Lucknow and Agra. Opened in a 600 sq ft space, the menu will feature local delicacies, where the emphasis will be on nutrition as well. The decor inside the cafe has shades of orange and red to mark the struggles of women and has been conceptualised by the Visual Arts Department of the Banaras Hindu University.

Address :- GPS Building, Sankat Mochan Rd, opposite Tulsi Manas Mandir, Tulsi Manas Mandir Colony, Naria, Varanasi, Uttar Pradesh 221010

DO CHECK THIS OUT NEXT TIME YOU VISIT UDAIPUR NEXT YEAR!



4. The Orange Cafe, Udaipur:-




Udaipur, the city of lakes where the team will open an outlet very soon. The women here are undergoing training where the chefs, waiters, and the billing personnel will be acid attack survivors.

It's under construction!! Will be ready by 2021.

DO CHECK THIS OUT NEXT TIME YOU VISIT VARANASI!
